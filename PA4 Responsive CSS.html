John Cena

Where is he from? West Newbury, Massachusetts, USA

How did I hear about him? I first heard about John Cena through his career in WWE as a wrestler and then in Hollywood as an actor.

Why do I want to meet him? John Cena is known for his dedication to fitness, his positive attitude, and philanthropy, particularly through the Make-A-Wish Foundation. He's an inspiring individual for his work ethic and charitable contributions.

Robert Wadlow

Where is he from? Alton, Illinois, USA

How did I hear about him? I learned about Robert Wadlow because he is recognized as the tallest man in recorded history.

Why do I want to meet him? His extraordinary height, combined with his calm demeanor and perseverance despite health challenges, makes him a fascinating historical figure.

Julius Caesar

Where is he from? Rome, Italy

How did I hear about him? Julius Caesar is one of the most well-known Roman emperors, famous for his military conquests and reforms that greatly impacted Roman society.

Why do I want to meet him? I want to understand his strategic mind, leadership abilities, and how he handled the politics of ancient Rome during his reign.
